Our Story

Rooted in tradition, shaped for today.

Meklit Edetibeb celebrates Ethiopian craftsmanship — from Jebena and Shekla Dist to contemporary sculptures. We partner with local artisans to create functional, beautiful pieces for homes, ceremonies, and gifts.

How we began

Founded in 2025 in Addis Ababa, our small studio started with a simple goal: make authentic pieces accessible with fair pricing and reliable delivery. What began as family craft grew into a community of makers.

Our approach

We design with purpose, refine with customer feedback, and keep production local. Every order supports our artisans and preserves traditional techniques while embracing modern quality standards.

Our Values

Authenticity

Traditional forms and finishes, made by Ethiopian hands using time-honored techniques.

Fair partnership

We pay fairly and reinvest in training, tools, and safety for all our artisans.

Quality

Consistent sizing, careful firing, and durable packaging to ensure your pieces arrive perfectly.

Sustainability

Local clay sourcing and reduced-waste production methods to minimize environmental impact.

The Craft Process

1) Clay selection

We work with regional clays known for heat resistance and beautiful finish when fired.

2) Forming

Hand-building and wheel throwing techniques, depending on the piece and tradition.

3) Drying & firing

Slow drying to prevent cracks; controlled firing for strength and beautiful coloration.

4) Finishing

Burnishing, engraving, and polishing for lasting beauty and authentic Ethiopian patterns.
