Our Process

1) Consultation

Share photos or sketches, sizes, quantity, budget, and timeline. We listen to understand your vision completely.

2) Quote & Sample

We confirm price and lead time. For larger orders, we create a sample piece for your approval before full production.

3) Production

Crafting, drying, firing, finishing, engraving/branding if needed. Each piece receives meticulous attention.

4) Delivery

Addis same-day courier; Ethiopia-wide shipping 2–5 days. International delivery available on request.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do custom orders take?

Small runs: 5–10 days. Larger/engraved orders: 10–21 days. We'll confirm exact timing after consultation.

Is Shekla Dist stove-safe?

Yes — start on low heat and increase gradually to avoid thermal shock. Our pieces are fired for durability.

Do you ship outside Addis?

Yes, 2–5 days to major cities. Packaging is reinforced with double boxing to prevent damage during transit.

Do you accept online payments?

We're adding Chapa soon. For now: cash on delivery or bank transfer for your convenience.
